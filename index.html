Food Waste and Insecurity Data

This project explores global food waste and insecurity data through interactive visualizations.

Food Waste Comparison by Country

Explore food waste volumes by sector (household, retail, food service) for countries with high-confidence estimates.

Food Insecurity Over Time

Visualize changes in food insecurity levels (moderately and severely food insecure) across different years and countries.

More Insights

Learn about the relationship between food insecurity and dietary energy requirements across the globe.

Project created by Nicholas Shor and Zoey Zhao.
